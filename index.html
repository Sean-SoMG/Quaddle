Quaddle Solo Digital

How to Play

The Board

Two players take turns placing Tetromino-shaped pieces on a 7×7 grid — you vs a digital opponent.

Your Turn

Each turn you draw a card showing the piece you must place:

  • Use ←→↑↓ to position the piece, or click / tap a board square directly
  • Press Rotate 90° to rotate before placing
  • Press Place to commit the move

Scoring

Fill an entire row or column with only your pieces → score 1 point and that line clears.

Phase 2 — Capture Mode

Unlocks when a player can't fit their piece on the board in Phase 1. Both players then play under Capture rules:

  • Opponent's squares → captured (become your colour)
  • Your own squares → flipped to opponent's colour — be careful!
  • Max 3 opponent squares may be captured per piece

Winning

  • First to 5 points wins immediately
  • If the deck runs out, highest score wins
  • If scores are tied, most pieces on the board wins
